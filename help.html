Help & Support

How can I place an order?

To place an order, simply browse our menu, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. Fill in your details and confirm your order.

What are the delivery charges?

Delivery charges vary based on your location. You'll see the exact delivery fee during the checkout process.

How do I track my order?

You can track your order status by logging into your account and visiting the 'My Orders' section.

What is your refund policy?

If you have any issues with your order, please contact our support team within 24 hours for assistance and possible refunds.
